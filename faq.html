FAQ

Questions offices usually ask before they start.

These answers are designed to make the decision easier and help you figure out whether Stella Market is the right fit for your breakroom.

Can we try the service before we commit?+

Yes. Stella Market starts with a free 2-week office trial so your team can experience the drinks, the setup, and the fit in your actual breakroom.

What kinds of offices are the best fit?+

The sweet spot is usually offices of 10 to 50 people, but Stella Market can service larger offices up to roughly 100 when the setup and usage profile make sense.

Do you offer real milk drinks?+

Yes. Real milk is a core part of the program for lattes, cappuccinos, flat whites, and similar drinks.

Do you offer cold brew?+

Yes. Real cold brew can be added as a separate premium beverage option for teams that want a broader menu.

Are your beans roasted locally?+

That is the goal whenever possible. Stella Market highlights locally roasted beans from St. Charles and the broader St. Louis region.

Do we have to use plastic k-cups?+

Stella Market believes in the quality of bean-to-cup coffee, but for companies that need to pair our premium bean-to-cup machines with K-cups, Stella Market can provide K-cups on a case-by-case basis and customize our coffee offerings.

What does our team need to handle each day?+

Very little. There may be light daily tasks like a milk rinse or simple grounds management, while larger support and maintenance stays with Stella Market.

Can Stella replace our current coffee setup?+

It can either complement what you already have or become your primary premium coffee option, depending on your office and preferences.

How do we get started?+

Start with the intake form or book a short consultation. From there we can confirm fit, timing, and the best trial setup for your office.

Do you serve St. Charles only?+

No. Stella Market serves St. Charles, St. Louis, and the Greater St. Louis region.

Still have questions?

We can walk through your office, your team size, and whether a free trial makes sense.
