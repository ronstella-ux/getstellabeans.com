Frequently asked questions

Common questions about office coffee, cold brew, and service.

Here are a few of the most common questions offices ask Stella Market & Bean Co.

What is bean-to-cup and why does it matter?+

Bean-to-cup is the ultimate in fresh coffee. Stella Market & Bean Co’s machines grind whole beans individually for each cup, giving drinkers a tailored cup of coffee with improved flavor, quality, and freshness.

Do your machines use real milk?+

Yes. Stella Market builds its premium latte and cappuccino experience around Jura machines that use real milk, delivering the ultimate in creamy texture and milk foam—just like your favorite barista, and a step above the chain coffee shops on every corner.

Can Stella Market & Bean Co customize coffee flavor profiles for each office?+

Yes. Stella Market & Bean Co can work with each office to build a coffee flavor profile they truly enjoy, with a responsive and customized approach built around your team’s tastes and preferences.

Do you offer cold brew?+

Yes. Stella Market can add compact cold brew coffee solutions that work well in office breakrooms and pair nicely with the main coffee station.

Are your beans local?+

Local sourcing matters to Stella Market. We highlight locally roasted beans from St. Charles and the broader St. Louis area whenever possible.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes. Stella Market offers a free 2-week office trial so you can see how the setup fits your team and your breakroom before moving forward.

Do we have to use plastic K-cups?+

Stella Market believes in the quality of bean-to-cup coffee, but for companies that need to pair our premium bean-to-cup machines with K-cups, Stella Market can provide K-cups on a case-by-case basis and customize our coffee offerings.

What size office is a good fit?+

Many Stella Market clients will be offices of roughly 10 to 50 employees, and we can often support offices up to about 100 depending on drink preferences, usage patterns, and the breakroom setup.

Do you only provide coffee?+

No. Stella Market can also discuss premium office water systems and breakroom pantry solutions as part of a broader employee amenity program.

We can walk through your office, your team size, and whether a free trial makes sense.
