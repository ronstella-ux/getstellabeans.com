Privacy policy

Simple privacy information for Stella Market & Bean Co.

A minimal privacy page for website visitors and offices reaching out about service.

Information we collect

If you contact Stella, book a call, or fill out the intake form, we may receive the information you provide, such as your name, email, company details, office size, and scheduling preferences.

How we use it

We use that information to respond to your inquiry, evaluate whether Stella is a fit for your office, and coordinate consultation or trial discussions.

Sharing

Stella does not sell your information. Information may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the website, scheduling tools, or communications.

Questions

If you have privacy questions or want us to update or delete information you shared directly with us, email info@getstellabeans.com.
