Cold brew coffee for offices

Real cold brew on tap, built for modern breakrooms.

Stella Market & Bean Co offers a compact, commercial-grade cold brew coffee solution designed to sit cleanly on a countertop and give offices a premium second beverage option beyond hot coffee.

Designed for bag-in-a-box cold brew coffee

The HK-46-BIB is specifically designed to dispense bag-in-a-box cold brew coffee in a clean, efficient office footprint.

Sleek and compact

Its compact cabinet fits up to one 3-gallon bag-in-a-box and sits comfortably on a counter or in small office spaces.

Nitro-style body without a tank

A unique motor creates nitro dispense without the need for a separate nitrogen tank, adding body and richness to the coffee.

Commercial-grade direct draw kit

The included premium direct draw kit uses food-grade, high-quality dispense parts backed by industry experience and service support.

Compact cold brew station

A polished countertop setup for offices that want a premium iced coffee experience without giving up valuable space.

Key specifications

A compact system with commercial-grade performance.
